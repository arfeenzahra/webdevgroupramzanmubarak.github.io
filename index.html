Ramzan Kareem

Ramadan Kareem' is the Arabic greeting phrase which means to have a blessed Ramadan or Happy Ramadan. The meaning of Ramadan Kareem is 'may Ramadan be generous to you.
Ramadan Kareem also refers to the Generous month of Ramadan. It is an expression that is used to greet and pray.
